🎉 Major Jewish Holidays

  • Rosh Hashanah – Jewish New Year celebrated with apples and honey for a sweet new year!
  • Yom Kippur – Day of Atonement, fast all day to apologize for wrongdoings and reflect
  • Hanukkah – Celebration of defeating the Greeks and reclaiming Jerusalem and the holy temple, when we eat sufganiot and light the hannukiah 🕎
  • Purim – Costumes and celebration of Jewish bravery and perserverence in the story of Esther
  • Passover – Celebrating the escape of the Jews from slavery in Egypt
  • Shavuot – Commemorating the giving of the Torah and tradition of eating lots of dairy meals
